Learn German!

With Deutschy, you will learn all the vocabulary you need to become a German native speaker. It has a minimalistic and fun design so you never get tired. It took me a lot to get to this app version!

By Vicken Pool

April 21, 2023

Google Play

I am a Flutter app developer, so this app was made with flutter! It took me around 300 hours of pure programming and CSV management, you know, adding verbs, better translations, etc. I am happy with the result, many more updates are on their way and better designs.


This proyect started because I want to study my bachelor in a German university, I already spoke fluent German but not at the level to study in the university. So in order to get that C1 level, I needed to learn all kinds of not common and specific verbs and words. So I developed this app that goes from A1 to C2 German vocabulary.


If you are a complete beginner or an advanced German speaker, download it! There is always a word in German to learn, it does not matter your current level. I hope I can share my passion about languages with all of you, and help those German language learners improve with my app!
